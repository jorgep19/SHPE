About SHPE-UF app

This application was created for, but not limited to, University of Florida SHPE members. The app is a handy database that offers a quick look at any company you wish to obtain information about. Each company has three major subdivisions. At a Glance will offer the user a quick overview of the company, this includes what industry they belong to, a brief explanations of the company's history, and their contact information. The second subdivision is the Company Summary and it consists of a more in depth description of what can be found in At a Glance and some other key pieces of information like what kind of majors are they looking to employ. Lastly we have the News subdivision, which is a link to the latest news about the company you are researching.

In here you will have direct access to every SHPE announcement being posted on Twitter and Facebook, making it easier for you to keep track of things. Also, you are being provided with a detailed information on SHPE's E-Board members, stalking has never been simpler. Last but certanly not least, we provide our Developer Team's contact information in case you want to know more about the app or whish to tell us how to improve your experience as a user, we understand that there is always room for improvement and your opinion is valued.

Special recognition goes to Erik Rodriguez, Project Director; Jorge Paez, Architect and Developer; Horacio Delgado, Developer; Irene Freire, Developer; Daniel Rincon, Developer; Daniel Fuentes, Developer; ... and others...

Thank you for using SHPE-UF app.
